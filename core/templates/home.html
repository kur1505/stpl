Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is My Account Number/User Id ?

    Account number is a unique number assigned for your GTPL billing account. You can check your account number on your GTPL statement.

  • I Have Forgotten My Login Password,What Do I Do Now ?

    Go to https://stpl.sadhanagroup.org/Login Click "Forgot Password". Enter your email id which is registered for self-care account. The password will be sent to your registered mobile number and email address.

  • Can The Name On The STPL Account Be Changed ?

    Minor change in spelling of your name as it appears on the account: This can be requested for through an email to our helpdesk team with an ID proof document showing the intended spelling.

  • One Moment Please.. ?

    Reason : Set top box getting ready for working
    Solution : Remove all favorite channels and Reboot STB
    Solution : After all process, facing same issue then call to customer care : Toll free - 1800-419-0419 | Toll No : 9727-633-633 and book complain.

  • Loading....?

    Reasone : Set top box getting ready for working
    Solution : Reboot STB

  • Error-4,6

    Reason : Channel Unavailable in particular package
    Solution : Check package details
    Solution : After all process, facing same issue then call to customer care : Toll free - 1800-419-0419 | +91 9727-633-633 and book complain

  • Remote Not Working

    Reasone : Battery cell dead
    Solution : Change battery Cell
    Reasone : Remote dead
    Solution : Purchase new remote

  • The Set Box is Unable to receive a Signal(No Signal)

    Reason : Cable unplugged with set top box
    Solution : Reboot STB
    Reason : : Cable cut down OR Junction problem / electrical problem
    Solution : Check cable connection or After all process, facing same issue then call to customer care : Toll free - 1800-419-0419 | Toll No : 9727-633-633 and book complain.

  • Please Insert your Smartcard,do not remove the card it is told to do so (Error-12)

    Reasone : Smartcard not inserted properly.
    Solution : Remove card & reinsert it or Card shall be up side downward OR Press card for proper adjustment.
    Solution : After all process, facing same issue then call to customer care : Toll free - 1800-419-0419 | Toll No : 9727-633-633 and book complain.

  • Error-11,14

    Solution : After all process, facing same issue then call to customer care : Toll free - 1800-419-0419 | Toll No : 9727-633-633 and book complain.

  • Still pictures (ALL STB)

    Reasone : RF cable not connected properly or loose/damaged.
